How to make Grilled Pizza Bianca

The Recipe

Prepared pizza dough allows the cook to grill a tasty pizza bianca, or white pizza, in under 30 minutes. Why call out for delivery when you can make an incredible pizza at home?
  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Paremesan Cheese
  • Dough(Duhh)

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds; stir in cream.
  2. Meanwhile, preheat a gas grill with all burners on High for 10 to 15 minutes
  3. Divide pizza dough in half. Shape each portion of the pizza dough into an 8 to 10-inch disc. Reduce the flame on half the grill burners to Low, to create areas of direct and indirect heat.
  4. Turn crust ninety degrees (do not flip dough over). Grill until crust shows light grill marks, 1 to 3 minutes.
  5. Grill until cheese is melted, about 3 minutes. Carefully remove pizza from the grill.
