Reuben Sandwich

A traditional reuben sandwich consists of thinly sliced corned beef, Swiss cheese, and sauerkraut on rye bread slathered with Russian or Thousand Island dressing.

The Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Steps

Ingredients

  • Rye bread
  • Thousand Island dressing
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Sliced corned beef
  • Softened Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a large griddle or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread one side of bread slices evenly with Thousand Island dressing. On four bread slices, layer one slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut, and a second slice of Swiss cheese.
  3. Place sandwiches, butter-side down on the preheated griddle; butter the top of each sandwich with remaining butter. Grill until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side
  4. Server hot and.. Enjoy!!
